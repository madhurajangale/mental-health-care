About Us

The mental health care website focuses on providing support for individuals dealing with depression, stress, and anxiety. It offers a personalized dashboard where users can assess their mental health through MCQs and receive tailored recommendations. These include books, exercises, and calming music based on their results. The platform aims to create a safe space for users to understand their emotional state and seek guidance. Its goal is to promote self-care and mental well-being by connecting people with effective resources.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Who is this quiz for?
A:The quiz is for anyone looking to assess their mental health, particularly those experiencing symptoms of depression, anxiety, or stress. It provides insights and personalized recommendations to help manage their well-being..

Q: Can I take the assessment multiple times?
A:Yes, you can retake the assessment as often as you like. It can be helpful to see how your mental state changes over time..
